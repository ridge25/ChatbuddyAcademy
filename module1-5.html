Emotional Detection

Before we finish up, let's make sure your bot is capable of understanding the mood you're in.

We can do this by adding more 'elif' statements, similar to the other ones we've coded to this point. Before we dive into the entire code, here are the two pieces that will allow your bot to understand emotions better...

# After combined greeting
print(f"ChatBuddy: {time_greeting}, {user_name}! {random.choice(greetings)} Ask me anything. 😄")
            
# Ask how they’re doing
print("ChatBuddy: How are you doing today?")
emotion_input = input(f"{user_name}: ").lower()
            
if "good" in emotion_input or "great" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: That's awesome to hear! 😄 Keep up the good vibes!")
elif "sad" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: I'm sorry you're feeling sad. I'm here if you want to talk. 💙")
elif "tired" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: You should try to rest up. Sleep is important! 😴")
elif "hungry" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: You better grab a snack! 🍕 Got a favorite food?")
elif "not great" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: I'm sorry you're not feeling great. Anything I can do to cheer you up? 🤗")
else:
    print("ChatBuddy: Thanks for sharing. I'm here for you no matter what! 😊")

Note that the only included emotions right now are 'good' , 'great' , 'sad' , 'tired' , 'hungry' & 'not great.' Any other response that is NOT included in this list will result in the 'else' response of:

"ChatBuddy: I'm sorry you're not feeling great. Anything I can do to cheer you up? 🤗"

You can add other emotions by utilizing the same structure as the one above!

Using the additional 'elif' statements we talked about before (⬆️), we can now integrate them with our current code!

import random
from datetime import datetime
            
print("🤖 Hello! I'm ChatBuddy. What's your name?")
user_name = input("You: ")
            
# Time of day greeting
current_hour = datetime.now().hour
            
if current_hour < 12:
    time_greeting = "Good morning"
elif current_hour < 18:
    time_greeting = "Good afternoon"
else:
    time_greeting = "Good evening"
            
# Greeting list
greetings = ["Hey there! 👋", "Hiya! 😊", "Howdy! 🤠", "What's up? 🙌", "Yo yo yo! 🤖"]
mood_responses = ["I'm doing great, thanks for asking! 😎", "Better now that you're here! 😁", "Feeling fantastic! What about you? 🌟"]
no_response = [
    "Hmm... I don't know how to respond to that yet. 🤔",
    "I'm still learning! Try something else. 📚",
    "That one's a mystery to me. 😅"
]
            
# Combined greeting
print(f"ChatBuddy: {time_greeting}, {user_name}! {random.choice(greetings)} Ask me anything. 😄")
            
# Ask how they’re doing and respond
print("ChatBuddy: How are you doing today?")
emotion_input = input(f"{user_name}: ").lower()
            
if "good" in emotion_input or "great" in emotion_input or "happy" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: That's awesome to hear! 😄 Keep up the good vibes!")
elif "sad" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: I'm sorry you're feeling sad. I'm here if you want to talk. 💙")
elif "tired" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: You should try to rest up. Sleep is important! 😴")
elif "hungry" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: You better grab a snack! 🍕 Got a favorite food?")
elif "not great" in emotion_input:
    print("ChatBuddy: I'm sorry you're not feeling great. Anything I can do to cheer you up? 🤗")
else:
    print("ChatBuddy: Thanks for sharing. I'm here for you no matter what! 😊")
            
# Begin chat loop
memory = {}
            
while True:
    user_input = input(f"{user_name}: ").lower()
            
    if "hello" in user_input or "hi" in user_input:
        print(f"ChatBuddy: {random.choice(greetings)}")
    elif "how are you" in user_input:
        print(f"ChatBuddy: {random.choice(mood_responses)}")
    elif "what time is it" in user_input or "current time" in user_input or "tell me the time" in user_input or "what's the time" in user_input:
        current_time = datetime.now().strftime("%I:%M %p")
        print(f"ChatBuddy: It's currently {current_time} ⏰")
    elif "what's the date" in user_input or "what is the date" in user_input or "current date" in user_input:
        current_date = datetime.now().strftime("%A, %B %d, %Y")
        print(f"ChatBuddy: Today is {current_date} 📅")
    elif "my favorite color is" in user_input:
        color = user_input.split("is")[-1].strip()
        memory["favorite_color"] = color
        print(f"ChatBuddy: Got it! I'll remember your favorite color is {color}. 🎨")
    elif "what's my favorite color" in user_input or "what is my favorite color" in user_input:
        if "favorite_color" in memory:
            print(f"ChatBuddy: Your favorite color is {memory['favorite_color']}! 🌈")
        else:
            print("ChatBuddy: Hmm... I don’t know yet. Tell me your favorite color! 🤷‍♂️")
    elif "my favorite food is" in user_input:
        food = user_input.split("is")[-1].strip()
        memory["favorite_food"] = food
        print(f"ChatBuddy: Yum! I'll remember your favorite food is {food}. 😋")
    elif "what's my favorite food" in user_input or "what is my favorite food" in user_input:
        if "favorite_food" in memory:
            print(f"ChatBuddy: I remember this one! Your favorite food is {memory['favorite_food']}! 🍽️")
        else:
            print("ChatBuddy: Hmm... I don't think you've told me your favorite food yet. Tell me what it is! 🤤")
    elif "my nickname is" in user_input:
        nickname = user_input.split("is")[-1].strip()
        memory["nickname"] = nickname
        print(f"ChatBuddy: That's a cool nickname! I'll remember it—{nickname}, right? 😎")
    elif "what's my nickname" in user_input or "what is my nickname" in user_input:
        if "nickname" in memory:
            print(f"ChatBuddy: You told me this one! Your nickname is {memory['nickname']}! 😄")
        else:
            print("ChatBuddy: Hmm... I don't think you've told me your nickname yet. Wanna share it? 🕵️")
    elif "bye" in user_input:
        print(f"ChatBuddy: Bye {user_name}! Catch you later! 👋")
        break
    else:
        # Optional: emotional check during the chat
        if any(word in user_input for word in ["good", "great", "sad", "tired", "hungry", "not great"]):
            if "good" in user_input or "great" in user_input:
                print("ChatBuddy: That’s awesome! 😄 Keep being you!")
            elif "sad" in user_input:
                print("ChatBuddy: I'm here for you if you need a virtual hug. 🤗")
            elif "tired" in user_input:
                print("ChatBuddy: Don’t push yourself too hard. Rest is key. 😴")
            elif "hungry" in user_input:
                print("ChatBuddy: You deserve a snack! 🍪")
            elif "not great" in user_input:
                print("ChatBuddy: Rough day? I'm here to chat. 💬")
        else:
            print(f"ChatBuddy: {random.choice(no_response)}")
